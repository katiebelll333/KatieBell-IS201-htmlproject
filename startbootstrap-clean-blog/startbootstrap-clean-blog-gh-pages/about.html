About Me

This is who I am.

My name is Katie Bell. I grew up here in Orem, Utah near the local Mountain View High School. I grew up as the second oldest child of six. I have an older brother, a younger sister, and following her, three younger brothers. There was never a dull moment in my home growing up.

As a child, I loved to be creative. I would make homemade recipies, I would paint, dig in the dirt and make mudpies and all sorts of creations in the outdoors. My mom would take us hiking in the mountains near our home, and we loved to explore and see the world around us.

In Junior High and Highschool, I stayed very involved in extracurricular activities and even though things at home were rough with my parents getting a divorce, I kept busy with student council, swim team, tennis, and choir. In the later years of High School, my mom got re-married to Jared, who has five children of his own, making our family a family of thirteen!
